🧮 BUSINESS LOGIC IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

CALCULATION FLOW:
1. HR Score = Sum of 10 criteria (0-10 each) = 0-100
2. Peer Average = Sum(all peer scores) / Number of peers
3. Presentation Score = (HR Score + Peer Average) / 2
4. Total Penalty = Average penalty from peer-HR comparison
   - Penalty per peer = 0 if deviation ≤ ±5, else (difference - 5)
5. Final Score = Presentation Score - Total Penalty (min 0)

EXAMPLE:
  HR: 75
  Peers: [70, 84, 95, 95, 80]
  
  Results:
  - HR Score: 75
  - Peer Average: 84.8
  - Presentation: 79.9
  - Penalty: 6.8
  - Final: 73.1

UPSERT PATTERN:
  ✅ Unique constraint: [review_id, presenter_id]
  ✅ Database-enforced single record per presenter
  ✅ Auto-create on first evaluation
  ✅ Auto-update on subsequent evaluations
  ✅ Never creates duplicates
